Quantitative Analysis in Psychology using SPSS Statistics

Course Structure
PART 1
  • Introduction: definition, descriptive & inferential statistics, importance & applications.
  • Planning: types of research studies in psychology, parametric & non-parametric statistics.
  • Organizing: scoring through MS Excel, plugging the data in SPSS-S.
  • Refining: removing outliers using SPSS-S, normality testing in SPSS-S.
PART 2
  • Parametric testing in SPSS-S: T-test (2 independent samples), ANOVA (K independent samples), Pearson’s correlation.
  • Non-parametric testing in SPSS-S: Mann-Whitney U Test (2 independent samples), Kruskal Wallis H Test (K independent sample), Spearman’s correlation.
PART 3
  • Simple linear regression: simple  linear regression in SPSS-S, creating simple linear regression equation.
  • Multiple linear regression: analyzing multiple IV’s in SPSS-S.
Clarification of doubts and queries.
Comprehensive self-assessment: 20 easy comprehensive MCQs.

SKILLVIRE's

We at SKILLVIRE provide excellent psychological research guidance to bachelor’s and master’s students. This service is specially designed for students who have difficulty in researching by themselves and who need constant support along with learnings. With our professional and experienced guidance, you can complete your research work in time with the required standards and expectations.

  • We believe in empowering students so, we help, guide, and teach but, not ‘literally do’ the entire work (the way how the real guide works).
  • Research guidance at SKILLVIRE is unique as per the type of research a student proposes. It can be purely qualitative or purely quantitative.
  • We help in domain selection, research type selection, variable selection, sample selection, topic formulation and, methodology formulation.
  • We train the student in the professional technique of literature review.
  • We provide full document of the research papers having limited access (if needed), tools (if needed), help in calculating the statistics (if needed), and teach how to analyze the qualitative data (if needed).
  • The Student is taught how to frame the results, discussions, and references as well.
  • Once the research is complete, we help the student to prepare a manuscript from their work that can be submitted in a journal with good indexes and high impact factor, later for their research paper publication.

Seats for this service are limited but sufficient. To apply for this service contact the SKILLVIRE team through email or WhatsApp after which, you will receive a revert back with further details. The charge of research guidance for bachelors students is INR 3500/- and for masters students is INR 4500/-. Do read our policies.

SKILLVIRE's

We believe in helping students to acquire practical skills and real-time work exposure. So, interns from psychology, education, and allied disciplines are welcomed to experience and contribute to SKILLVIRE services. We follow the work-from-home model for our organization and, we have a morning shift and an evening shift for internship/ training. You can click on ‘APPLY NOW’ button, log in to your Google account, fill in the G-form, and submit.

Description

SKILLVIRE is a psychological research and education organization so, the internship/ training will be of that nature only. We constantly try to prepare educational content, manage delivery, and optimize services. We indulge in tasks associated with psychological research and education, and therefore, we always touch all the subdisciplines of psychology like clinical, counseling, organizational, military, sports, forensics, developmental, cognitive, etc. An intern’s development at SKILLVIRE depends upon their curiosity, dedication, communication, ability to learn, and making the most of the given resources.

Roles & responsibilities

  • General roles & responsibilities: these are common for the interns at SKILLVIRE. It includes attending team meetings, calculating quantitative data, collecting data, collecting literature, digital paperwork, research template building, finding tools, helping in services optimization & ideas, outreach, website supervision, external monitoring of the social media handles, etc.
  • Specific roles & responsibilities: these can be assigned based upon the availability & the capability of the interns. It includes client handling, service content management, movable-immovable graphics planning, policies & ethical planning, event planning & execution.

Applying

You can join anytime throughout the year for up to 100 hours at a time after which, you can reapply as per your requirement. An intern can dedicate either morning shift (11 am to 5 pm) or evening shift (5 pm to 10 pm) work from home commitment. This organization does not charge any fee or provide salary/ stipend for the internship or training but, the work experience/ training letter/ certificate is chargeable (covers the letterhead, signature, stamp, and design charges). Experience/ training letter/ certificate for up to the first 100 hours is INR 500. For up to every next 100 hours INR 250 is the charge for the experience/ training letter/ certificate. An internship offer letter is not a proof of internship completion at SKILLVIRE. There is a list of recognized interns who carry a proof of internship completion on policies page. Do read our policies before applying.
