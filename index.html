Frequently Asked Questions

What is SKILLVIRE?
SKILLVIRE is an educational support service platform which provides online education & research guidance.
What does SKILLVIRE do?
SKILLVIRE provides educational videos & psychological research guidance to Bachelor’s & Master’s students in India.
What is online education?
Online education is a form of education delivered to the recipients through the internet.
Is online education from SKILLVIRE credible?
Yes, it is credible. Firstly, we only prefer qualified and experienced trainers (completed their degrees from UGC-recognized institutions, carry valid certifications, published research work, & have practical work experience). Secondly, we follow UGC (University Grants Commission) Swayam’s four-quadrant guidelines for our premium online education content. Third, our evidence-based & application-focused approach makes online education an overall fit for the industry. Fourth, we are a platform registered with Ministry of Micro Small and Medium Enterprise, Govt of India.
How do I buy a course/ lecture/ educational content?
To buy a course/ lecture/ educational content, choose the course from the carousel & click on the ‘BUY NOW’ button. You may have to log in to your Google account to fill the G-form, & that’s it. Once you submit the form, you will get the links to access the course videos, resource material, & other instructions. Copy that whole text & save it. It may take a minute but less than 24 hours for those links to get activated. You will only be able to access them if you are logged in  to Google Drive & YouTube with the Google Id you submitted during the buying process. No extra account creation, no app downloads..!
What all do I get when I buy a course/ lecture/ educational content?
When you buy a course/ lecture/ educational content, you get lifetime access to all the course videos. You also get the presentation or any other reference material used in the video, along with the links for downloading the eBooks & research papers. For some educational content, you get practice sheets or files. We also provide an arrangement for clearing doubts & queries.

Lifetime access to the premium content & certificate are provided only to the registered participants.
Is there any system of attendance or evaluation for online education?
As we follow UGC Swayam guidelines for our premium content, you will also get a link to a short & easy quiz as a part of the self-assessment, along with the course material. These tests are not like any exam &, they are crafted carefully for learning. If your test gets submitted in time & has a score of >35% then you will receive your certificate & scorecard within 3 working days. You can always take reattempts. For the events, you must complete the attendance only.
When do I get my certificate for online education?
For educational content other than events, watch all the tutorials & submit the test. As soon as your test gets submitted in time & has a score of >35% then you will receive your certificate & scorecard within 3 working days. You can always take reattempts. For an event, those who actively attend & fill in the attendance get a certificate within 10 working days. The certificate for a free event is different from the paid event. Paid event certificate has a unique certificate ID, a date of issuing stamp, & signatures.
What is research guidance?
Professional help, advice, or counseling, that is given to the students for completing their research or dissertation work.
How can I apply for research guidance at SKILLVIRE?
You can take advantage of our free basic-level guidance about how to initiate/ carry out a research in psychology by contacting us. To apply for the premium guidance service contact the SKILLVIRE through Email or WhatsApp after which, you will receive a revert back with further details. The charge of premium research guidance for both bachelors & masters students starts from INR 3800/- per research. Apart from premium research guidance, we also provide review research guidance (Starting from INR 950/- per research), quantitative data analysis service (Starting from INR 650/- per research), and qualitative data analysis service (Starting from INR 800/- per research).
How exactly do this research guidance works?
We believe in empowering students so, we help, guide, & teach but, not ‘literally do’ the entire work (the way how the real guide works). Research guidance at SKILLVIRE is optimized as per the type of research we engage in (qualitative or quantitative). We help in domain selection, research type selection, variable selection, sample selection, topic formulation, & methodology formulation. We train the student in the professional technique of literature review. We provide full document of the research papers having limited access (if needed), tools (if needed), help in calculating the statistics (if needed), & teach how to analyze the qualitative data (if needed). The Student is taught how to frame the results, discussions, & references as well. Once the research is complete, we help the student to prepare a manuscript from their work that can be submitted in a journal with good indexes and high impact factor, later for their research paper publication.
