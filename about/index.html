About Us

Peace and Happiness to you..!

“Thank you for your interest in reading more about SKILLVIRE..!”

SKILLVIRE is an online education and psychological research guidance platform. We believe in making every student skillful and industrial-ready.

For online education, we believe in two basic principles –

  • APPLICABILITY: One of the essential features of a good course/ webinar/ lecture is that it covers the applications of the concerned topic. Our content is designed with an application-focused mindset for the attendees to learn and use those acquired skills practically.
  • QUALITY: Another essential feature is content quality. with a professional trainer/resource person on the job together with evidence-based teaching results in an overall quality learning experience.

Our webinars and lectures are best and credible because firstly, we only prefer qualified and experienced trainers (completed their degrees from UGC-recognized institutions, carry valid certifications, published research work, or have practical work experience). Secondly, we follow UGC (University Grants Commission) Swayam’s four-quadrant guidelines for our premium online education. Courses on UGC SWAYAM are in four quadrants – (first) video lecture, (second) reading material that can be downloaded or printed, (third) self-assessment through tests or quizzes, and (fourth) an arrangement for clearing the doubts. Any student who buys a course from SKILLVIRE gets lifetime access to tutorial videos, all the educational resources, a comprehensive quiz for self-evaluation, and arrangements for asking or clearing any doubts.

As far as our research guidance is concerned, we believe in three basic principles –

  • INNOVATION: Good research is new and unique in terms of ideas.
  • DILIGENCE: Good research reflects the careful and persistent efforts of a researcher.
  • ETHICS: Good research is free from bias. It takes care of ethical approvals (if necessary), informed consent and carefully considers all other factors.

Good research is a bi-combination of creativity and rigor. It should have a strong rationale, methodology and, applications besides being evidence-based. When all these things are played under ethical boundaries, a good piece of knowledge is created. For our research guidance service, we follow student’s university prescribed guidelines.
