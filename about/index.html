About Us

Peace and Happiness to you..!

“Thank you for your interest in reading more about SKILLVIRE..!”

SKILLVIRE is an online education & psychological research guidance platform. We believe in making every student skillful & industrial-ready. We are registered as educational support service with the Ministry of Micro Small Medium Enterprise (MSME), Government of India.

For online education, we believe in two basic principles -

  • APPLICABILITY: One of the essential features of a good educational content is that it covers the applications of the concerned topic. Our content is designed with an application-focused mindset for the attendees to learn & use those acquired skills practically.
  • QUALITY: Another essential feature is content quality. with a professional trainer/resource person on the job together with evidence-based teaching results in an overall quality learning experience.

Our educational content is best & credible because firstly, we only prefer qualified & experienced trainers (completed their degrees from UGC-recognized institutions, carry valid certifications, published research work, or have practical work experience). Secondly, we follow UGC (University Grants Commission) Swayams four-quadrant guidelines for our premium online education. Courses on UGC SWAYAM are in four quadrants (first) video lecture, (second) reading material that can be downloaded or printed, (third) self-assessment through tests or quizzes, & (fourth) an arrangement for clearing the doubts. Any student who buys a course from SKILLVIRE gets lifetime access to tutorial videos, all the educational resources, a comprehensive quiz for self-evaluation, & arrangements for asking or clearing any doubts.

As far as our research guidance is concerned, we believe in three basic principles -

  • INNOVATION: Good research is new & unique in terms of ideas.
  • DILIGENCE: Good research reflects the careful & persistent efforts of a researcher.
  • ETHICS: Good research is free from bias. It takes care of ethical approvals (if necessary), informed consent & carefully considers all other factors.

Good research is a bi-combination of creativity & rigor. It should have a strong rationale, methodology &, applications besides being evidence-based. When all these things are played under ethical boundaries, a good piece of knowledge is created. For our research guidance service, we follow student’s university prescribed guidelines.
