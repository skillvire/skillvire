SKILLVIRE is an online education and psychological research guidance platform. We believe in making every student skillful and industrial-ready.<\/p>

    Our educational content is best and credible because firstly, we only prefer qualified and experienced trainers (completed their degrees from UGC-recognized institutions, carry valid certifications, published research work, or have practical work experience). Secondly, we follow UGC (University Grants Commission) Swayam\u2019s four-quadrant guidelines for our premium online education. Courses on UGC SWAYAM are in four quadrants \u2013 (first) video lecture, (second) reading material that can be downloaded or printed, (third) self-assessment through tests or quizzes, and (fourth) an arrangement for clearing the doubts. Any student who buys a course from SKILLVIRE gets lifetime access to tutorial videos, all the educational resources, a comprehensive quiz for self-evaluation, and arrangements for asking or clearing any doubts.<\/p>
