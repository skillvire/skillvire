INTRODUCTION<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

The SKILLVIRE is a part-time sole proprietorship, which was founded in March 2021 & launched in May 2021 by Rohan Rawat as an online\/offline education & training platform. We provide educational videos & psychological research guidance to bachelor’s & master’s students in India. Currently, the headquarters is in Sector 1, Vaishali, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India but, we operate on a work-from-home basis. Active work hours of SKILLVIRE are between 5 PM to 10 PM, Monday to Friday. Copyright of all the digital & non-digital content or material & services is restricted to use by SKILLVIRE only. At SKILLVIRE, we keep an official record of the number of sensitive documents released for each client in a separate database. Any redistribution or use of these sensitive documents without our knowledge is a punishable offense. There is a unique Id for each premium content certificate or letter for authentication\/ verification. To apply for our services, the user must have a Google account. Since July 2021, we have discontinued some of our free services.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

ONLINE EDUCATION<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Our online education services are powered by Google, Facebook, & JIO applications\/services. At SKILLVIRE, we provide online course\/ lecture\/ educational content on topics related to but not limited to psychology. We follow UGC (University Grants Commission) Swayam\u2019s four-quadrant guidelines for our premium online education content. Courses on UGC SWAYAM are in four quadrants \u2013 (first) video lecture, (second) reading material that can be downloaded or printed, (third) self-assessment through tests or quizzes, & (fourth) an arrangement for clearing the doubts. Any student who buys a course from SKILLVIRE gets lifetime access to tutorial videos, all the educational resources, a comprehensive quiz for self-evaluation, & arrangements for asking or clearing any doubts.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

To access free content, click on the ‘ACCESS FREE CONTENT’ button below to access free content. To buy a course\/ lecture\/ educational content, choose the course from the carousel & click on the \u2018BUY NOW\u2019 button. You may have to log in to your Google account to fill the G-form, & that\u2019s it. Once you submit the form, you will get the links to access the course videos, resource material, & other instructions. Copy that whole text & save it. It may take a minute but less than 24 hours for those links to get activated. You will only be able to access them if you are logged in  to Google Drive & YouTube with the Google Id you submitted during the buying process. No extra account creation, no app downloads..! As soon your test gets submitted within time & has a score of >35%, you will receive your certificate & scorecard within 3 working days. These tests are not like any exam &, they are crafted carefully for learning. Registration for any event generally starts around 15 days before & closes 3 days before the commencement. Details like event name, closing dates of registration, event links, & the price of the event are available below in the carousel. If you are interested in attending any event, you can click on the \u2018Register now\u2019 button & get yourself registered. All this information & links are available on our social media handles as well. Stay updated by following us on Instagram, Facebook, & LinkedIn. Subscribe to our YouTube channel to attend all the online events & access free content. Lifetime access to premium content & certificate are provided only to the registered participants. You will receive the certificate within 10 working days. The certificate for a free event is different from the paid event. A paid event certificate has a unique certificate ID, a date of issuing stamp, & signatures. The average course ratings which gets published for premium courses are given by the participants during the time of event hosting of that course.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

PSYCHOLOGICAL RESEARCH GUIDANCE<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

We at SKILLVIRE provide excellent psychological research guidance to bachelor\u2019s & master\u2019s students in India. This service is specially designed for students who have difficulty in researching by themselves & who need constant support along with learnings. With our professional & experienced guidance, you can complete your research work in time with the required standards & expectations.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We believe in empowering students so, we help, guide, & teach but, not \u2018literally do\u2019 the entire work (the way how the real guide works). Research guidance at SKILLVIRE is optimized as per the type of research we engage in (qualitative or quantitative). We help in domain selection, research type selection, variable selection, sample selection, topic formulation &, methodology formulation. We train the student in the professional technique of literature review. We provide a full document of the research papers having limited access (if needed), tools (if needed), help in calculating the statistics (if needed), & teach how to analyze the qualitative data (if needed). The Student is taught how to frame the results, discussions, & references as well. Once the research is complete, we help the student to prepare a manuscript from their work that can be submitted in a journal with good indexes & high impact factor, later for their research paper publication (we do not provide service of publishing a research paper). Seats for the research guidance services are limited but sufficient. You can take advantage of our free basic-level guidance about how to initiate\/ carry out a research in psychology by\u00a0contacting<\/a>\u00a0us. To apply for the premium guidance service\u00a0contact<\/a>\u00a0the SKILLVIRE through Email or WhatsApp after which, you will receive a revert back with further details. The charge of premium research guidance for both bachelors & masters students is INR 3500\/- per research. We provide one research with any number of authors per payment. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

REFUND POLICY<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Online education: Once registered for an event, payment is only fully refunded if the service is canceled by us or not fully delivered. There is no refund once educational content is bought from the SKILLVIRE. If you fail to submit the test with required criteria, you can retry. There is no refund once the certificate is issued. If anybody registers & does not actively attend the event (fails to complete the attendance), then the payment is refunded after a 10% deduction. There is no refund if the participant decides to leave an event at any point from registration up to the end. Registrations are not accepted if the payment is done on the wrong account. There is no refund in cases like wrong information, forgery, faking, & all others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Psychological research guidance: Once applied, payment is only fully refunded if the service is canceled by us or not fully delivered. If the client decides to end the service at any point between their payment & end of service mail, we refund the money after a 50% deduction. There is no refund once the \u2018end of service\u2019 mail is released. Service is not initiated if the payment is done on the wrong account. There are no refunds in cases like wrong information, forgery, faking, & all others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

DATA<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

At SKILLVIRE, we do not collect or store any personal information until the visitor applies for any service. A valid Google account is required to securely apply. None of the user’s information (which they don’t submit) gets saved or tracked. Our website has 0 trackers, 0 ads, no HTTPS upgrades, & 0 fingerprinting methods as per the epic browser’s report. We have a GTmetrix grade of ‘A’ for this static website. We do not use or sell any of our data or records of our clients. All the data is kept safely in an encrypted database that is accessible by the owner of SKILLVIRE only. Access to all the client data & social media handles is available to the owner of the SKILLVIRE only. Just like all other websites,\u00a0www.skillvire.ga<\/a>\u00a0also stores cookies & cache. You may be asked for your data like email, full name, date of birth, gender, city, country, institution\/ organization, designation, WhatsApp number, proof of payment, ratings, & opinions if you apply for any service.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

COMMUNICATION & SERVICES<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

SKILLVIRE is a small organization. If there is a delay in reverting, then possibly the member might be stuck in some urgent task or issue. The best is to wait for a revert or try again in some time. Saturday & Sunday are off but we remain potentially active on communication & services. Optimal service depends on cooperation, active conversations, task completions, network connections, & the accuracy of the data submitted by the applicant.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

DISCOUNTS & BENEFITS<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

We provide discounts & benefits in our services from time to time to the course participants, clients, interns, & employees. These may be subjected to changes, discontinuation, or cancellation if necessary. SKILLVIRE coupons will only be acceptable if all the commitments or dues (if any) are cleared.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
